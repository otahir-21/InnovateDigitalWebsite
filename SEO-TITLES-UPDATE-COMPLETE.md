## üéØ Title Optimization Strategy Used

### Formula Applied:
```
[Primary Keyword] [Location] | [Benefit/Secondary Keyword] | [Brand]
```

### Examples:
- ‚úÖ SEO Company Dubai | #1 SEO Services UAE | Innovate Digital
- ‚úÖ PPC Agency Dubai | Google Ads Management UAE | Innovate Digital
- ‚úÖ Best Digital Marketing Agency in Dubai | Innovate Digital

### Key Principles:
1. **Location First:** Dubai/UAE in every title
2. **Keywords:** Target exact match keywords (e.g., "SEO Company Dubai")
3. **Length:** 50-60 characters (perfect for Google)
4. **Brand:** Company name at end
5. **Compelling:** Use power words (Best, Top, #1, Leading)
