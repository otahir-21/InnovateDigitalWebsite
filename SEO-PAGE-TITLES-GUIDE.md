## ðŸ“ Title Tag Best Practices

### Character Limits:
- **Optimal Length:** 50-60 characters
- **Maximum:** 60 characters (Google truncates after this)
- **Minimum:** 30 characters

### Formula for Perfect Titles:

```
[Primary Keyword] [Location] | [Secondary Keyword/Benefit] | [Brand]

Examples:
âœ… SEO Company Dubai | Rank #1 on Google | Innovate Digital
âœ… Web Development Dubai | Custom Website Design | Innovate Digital
âœ… PPC Agency Dubai | Google Ads Experts | Innovate Digital
```

### Structure Priority:

1. **Primary Keyword** (Most important - left side)
   - Example: "SEO Company Dubai"

2. **Location** (If not in primary keyword)
   - Example: "Dubai", "UAE"

3. **Secondary Keyword/Benefit** (Middle)
   - Example: "Rank #1 on Google"

4. **Brand Name** (End)
   - Example: "| Innovate Digital"

2. **favicon-16x16.png** (16x16 px)

4. **apple-touch-icon.png** (180x180 px)
5. **android-chrome-192x192.png** (192x192 px)
6. **android-chrome-512x512.png** (512x512 px)

### How to Create:

**Option 1: Use Online Tool (Easiest)**
1. Go to: https://realfavicongenerator.net
2. Upload your logo (square, PNG, 512x512px minimum)
3. Download generated favicon package
4. Extract to `/public` folder

**Option 2: Design Tool**
1. Create square logo in Canva/Figma (1024x1024px)
2. Export as PNG
3. Use favicon generator to create all sizes

**Option 3: Manual (Advanced)**
1. Use Photoshop/Illustrator
2. Create each size manually
3. Export optimized files
